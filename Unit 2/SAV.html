Surface Area to Volume

The size of a cell will dictate the function.

Cells need a high surface area-to-volume ratio to optimize the exchange of material through the plasma membrane.

Formulas:
  • Cuboidal cells:
    • Surface Area = height x width x number of sides x number of boxes
    • Volume = length x width x height x number of boxes
  • Spherical cells:
    • Surface Area = 4πr2
    • Volume = 4⁄3πr3
