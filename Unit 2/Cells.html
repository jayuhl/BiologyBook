Cells

Cells: the basic structural and functional units of every organism

All cells:

  1. Are bound by a plasma membrane
  2. contain cytosol (cytoplasm)
  3. contain chromosomes
  4. contain ribosomes

There are two types of cells: prokaryotes and eukaryotes.

Prokayotes
  • Domains Bacteria and Archea
  • DNA is in nucleoid region
  • generally smaller in size than eukaryotes
Eukaryotes
  • Protists, fungi, animals, plants
  • DNA in nucleus
  • contain membrane-bound organelles
