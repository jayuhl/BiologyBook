Plant Cells

Plants have a cell wall that cover their plasma membranes

  • Extracellular structure (not fonud in animal cells)
  • Provides:
    • shape/structure
    • protection
    • regulation of water intake

The cell wall is composed of cellulose.

  • thicker than plasma membranes
  • contain plasmodesmata
    • Hole-like structure in cell wall filled with cytosol that connect adjacent cells
