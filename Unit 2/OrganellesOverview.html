Organelles Overview

Organelles: membrane-bound strctures in eukaryotes

Two Classifications:

  • Endomembranes Organelles
    1. Nuclear Envelope
    2. Endoplasmic Reticulum
    3. Golgi Complex
    4. Lysosomes
    5. Vesicles/Vacuoles
    6. Plasma Membrane
  • Energy Organelles
    1. Mitochondria
    2. Chloroplasts
Compartmentalization

Compartmentalization in organelles allows for different metabolic reactions to occur in different locations.

  • increases surface area for reactions to occur
  • prevents interfering reactions from occurring in the same location
