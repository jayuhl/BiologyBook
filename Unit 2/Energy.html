Mitochondria

Size of cellular respiration

  • Structure of the double membrane:
    • outer membrane is smooth
    • inner membrane has folds â†’ cristae
      • increases the surface area
    • divides the mitochondria into two internal compartments:
      1. Intermembrane: space between inner and outer membrane
      2. Mitchondrial Matrix: enclosed by innermembrane
        • Location for the Kreb's Cycle
        • Countains:
          • enzymes that catalyze cellular respiration and produce ATP
          • mitochondrial DNA

The number of mitochondria in a cell correlates with metabolic activity.

  • Cells with high metabolic activity have more mitochondria
Chloroplast

Specialized organelles in photosynthetic organisms

  • site of photosynthesis
  • contain green pigment chlorophyll

Inside of its double membrane:

  • Thylakoids
    • Membranous sac that can organize into stacks called grana
      • Light dependent reactions occur in grana
  • Stroma: fluid around thylakoids
    • Location for the Calvin Cycle
    • Countains:
      • chloroplast DNA
      • Ribosomes
      • Enzymes
