Plasma Membrane

Separates internal cell environment from external environment

  • Comprised primarily of phospholipids
  • Phospholipids are amphipathic
    • Hydrophylic head and hydrophobic tails
      • Hydrophilic head oriented towards aqueous environment
      • Hydrophobic tails facing inwards away from aqueous environments
    • forms a bilayer

Selective permeability: ability of membranes to regulate substances that enter and exit the cell
