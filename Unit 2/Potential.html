Water Potential

Water potential: physical property that predicts the direction water will flow

  • includes the effects of solute concentration of physical pressure
  • Water will flow from areas of high water potential to low water potential
Formula: Ψ = ΨS + ΨP
  • Ψ - Water Potential (megapascacls or bars)
  • ΨS - Solute Potential
  • ΨP - Pressure Potential

ΨS = -iCRT

  • ΨS - Solute Potential
  • i - ionization constant (number of particles formed)
  • C - molar concentration
  • R - Pressure Constant
    • 0.0831 liter bars/mol-K
    • 0.00831 liter MPA/mol-K
  • T - temperature in K (C° + 273)
