Metabolic Pathways

Metabolism

all of the chemical reactions in an organism

Metabolic Pathways

a series of chemical reactions that either build complex molecules or break down complex molecules

There are two types of metabolic pathways:


Catabolic Pathways
  • release energy by breaking down complex molecules into simpler molecules
  • Ex. cellular respiration
Anabolic Pathways
  • consume energy by building complicated molecules from simpler molecules
  • Ex. photosynthesis
