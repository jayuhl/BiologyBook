Laws of Thermodynamics

The study of energy transformations in matter is called thermodynamics.

The law applies to the universe as a whole

1st Law:
  • energy cannot be created or destroyed
  • energy is always transferred or transformed
  • Ex. Photosynthesis uses energy from the sun, which is transformed into chemical energy
2nd Law:
  • Energy transformation increase entropy in the universe.
  • During energy transformations, some of the enrgy is unusable and often lost as heat.
